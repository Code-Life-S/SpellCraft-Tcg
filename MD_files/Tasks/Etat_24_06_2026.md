## Architecture

- **Framework**: Vanilla JS, HTML5, CSS3
- **Stockage**: localStorage
- **Ecrans**: Menu Principal, Vague Defense, Deck Builder, Arena (Builder + Adventure)
- **Composants**: SpellCard, PlayerHand, EnemyBoard, VisualEffects, DeckTracker, ActionHistory

## Mecaniques implementees

- 16 sorts neutres, 15 sorts de classe (3x5 classes)
- Systeme de reactions elementaires (Melt, Overload, Shatter)
- Capacites ennemies (Provocation, Enrage, Soigneur, Invocateur)
- 3 Boss (Roi Squelette, Mage Noir, Dragon)
- XP / Niveaux / Deblocage cartes et classes
- Mode Arena (12 rounds, draft, upgrades)
- Compteur X/3 Archimage (affiche pres des PV)
- Bouton HP->Mana OmbreLumiere (affiche pres des PV)
- Mots-cles: Copy, Recall, Lifesteal
